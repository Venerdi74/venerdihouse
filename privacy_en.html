Privacy and Cookie Policy

Last update: 01/07/2025.

Pursuant to Article 13 of EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR), we provide the following information regarding the processing of personal data collected through the Venerdì House website.

1. Types of data collected

The data collected through the website may include:

  • Identification data (first and last name)
  • Contact details (email, phone)
  • Booking request details (check-in and check-out dates, number of guests, any messages)
  • Technical navigation data (e.g., IP address, user agent, access logs)

2. Purposes and legal basis of processing

Data collected is processed for the following purposes:

  • Managing availability and booking requests → legal basis: performance of pre-contractual or contractual measures
  • Legal and tax obligations → legal basis: legal obligations
  • Website security and prevention of abuse → legal basis: legitimate interest
  • Sending promotional communications (if applicable) → legal basis: consent

3. Processing methods

Data is processed both in paper and digital formats, in compliance with the security measures required by current legislation.

4. Data recipients

Data may be disclosed to external parties (e.g., tax consultants, IT providers) only when strictly necessary and always in compliance with the principle of data minimization.

5. Transfer of data to non-EU countries

Any transfers of data outside the EU will take place in compliance with the safeguards provided by Articles 44 et seq. of the GDPR.

6. Data retention period

Data will be retained for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes indicated and, in any case, no longer than the timeframes established by law.

7. Cookies and tracking tools

This website uses various types of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Technical cookies
    Necessary for the website’s operation and for ensuring security and access to core functionalities. These do not require user consent.
  • Analytics cookies
    Collect information in aggregate and anonymous form about website usage (e.g., pages visited, time spent) to improve service quality. These are used only with your consent, if required.
  • Third-party cookies
    May be set by external services integrated into the website (e.g., maps, social media buttons). The use of these cookies requires your prior consent unless they are strictly technical. In particular, the website integrates the interactive Google Maps service. This service may set third-party cookies and collect navigation data even if the user does not directly interact with it. The map is blocked for privacy reasons and is only loaded if the user clicks the “Accept the map” button. In this way, any cookies or browsing data are transmitted to Google Maps only after the user’s explicit consent.

Third parties that may set cookies through this website include, for example:

  • Google Maps → Read Google’s Privacy Policy
  • Instagram → Read Instagram’s Privacy Policy
  • Facebook → Read Facebook’s Privacy Policy

You can give or refuse consent for non-technical cookies through the cookie banner shown when accessing the website. You can also manage your cookie preferences:

  • via your browser settings (e.g., blocking or deleting saved cookies)
  • through any consent banners present on the website

Blocking cookies entirely or partially may affect the correct functioning of some parts of the website.

8. Data subject rights

Users have the right to:

  • access their personal data
  • obtain correction or deletion of their data
  • request restriction of processing
  • object to processing
  • receive their data in a structured format (data portability)
  • withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent given before its withdrawal
  • lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority

9. Changes to this policy

The Controller reserves the right to modify this policy at any time. Any updates will be published on this page.

10. Contact information

For any requests or information, you can write to venerdihouse@gmail.com.

11. Data controller
