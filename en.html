About Us

Venerdì House is a holiday apartment located in the heart of Alghero, perfect for families and couples. Just minutes from the historic center and steps away from San Giovanni beach. A place where you will feel right at home.

Plan your stay in Sardinia now and enjoy exclusive rates when you book directly on our website.

Our Apartment

The Venerdì House apartment offers a spacious 80 sqm layout, located on the second floor (no elevator). It includes two bedrooms, a living room with a fully equipped kitchen, bathroom, and balconies. Equipped with air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, and all the comforts for a pleasant stay in Alghero.
